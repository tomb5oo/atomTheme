Student Accomodation Chester

The Hammond School is the North West’s leading provider of Performing Arts education and the UK’s oldest vocational dance school, offering vocational training in dance, drama and music, alongside an enriching academic curriculum. Housed in the historic Hoole Bank House and surrounded by beautifully landscaped grounds, the campus comprises a Performing Arts Centre with a 420-seat theatre and state-of-the-art dance and music studios.

The Hammond School, Chester

The Hammond School is situated on the outskirts of Chester, in an area called Hoole Village. Hoole Village is a rural area of Chester, surrounded by farm land and horse paddocks. Hoole Village is an 8 minute drive from Chester City centre (2.5 miles), and just a short walk from the suburb Hoole (1.5 miles). Hoole has been named the Best Local Centre in the country, when it won Great British High Street award win in 2016 and has everything you could want, including Co-op, Sainsbury's, Costa Coffee, as well as many restaurants, cafe's, independent shops, a butches, a delicatessen, pubs and wine bars
